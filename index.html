South Carolina is one of the leading nuclear energy producers in the United States. Nuclear power plays a major role in providing reliable, affordable, and carbon-free electricity to homes, schools, businesses, and industries across the state.

This website explores how nuclear energy works, where South Carolina’s nuclear facilities are located, the impacts of nuclear power on our state, and how it compares to other energy sources. You can even test your knowledge with our interactive quiz!

Why Nuclear Matters

Supports SC Citizens

Nuclear energy is South Carolina’s largest source of electricity generation.

Low Carbon Emissions

Nuclear power produces electricity without releasing carbon dioxide during operation.

Reliable 24/7 Energy

Nuclear facilities provide jobs and contribute significantly to local and state economies.
